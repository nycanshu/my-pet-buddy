Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

At My Pet Buddy, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our browser extension.

Our Commitment to Privacy

We believe in transparency and simplicity. My Pet Buddy is designed with privacy in mind - we don't collect, store, or transmit any personal information about you.

Data Collection

My Pet Buddy does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The extension is designed to work entirely locally on your device without sending any data to external servers.

Local Storage

The extension uses your browser's local storage to remember your preferences, including:

  • Your selected pet companion
  • Pet position on screen (top, middle, or bottom)
  • Extension settings and preferences

This data remains exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third parties.

No Tracking or Analytics

We do not use any tracking technologies, analytics tools, or data collection services. Your browsing activity, websites visited, or any other information is not monitored or recorded.

Permissions Explained

My Pet Buddy requires minimal permissions to function:

  • Storage: To save your pet preferences locally
  • ActiveTab: To inject pet animations on the current webpage
  • Content Scripts: To display your pet companion on websites

These permissions are used solely for the extension's core functionality and do not involve data collection.

Third-Party Services

My Pet Buddy does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or data collection tools. The extension operates independently without external dependencies.

Children's Privacy

Our extension is safe for users of all ages. Since we don't collect any personal information, there are no special considerations for children's privacy beyond our standard no-data-collection policy.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
